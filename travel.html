Travelling

Travelling is a major passion of mine! I have travelled 3 countries so far, some multiple times!
I really want to travel to every country at some stage in my life. My Dad has lived in Australia, Germany, New Zealand, England, Thailand and many more so I think that is where my love for travelling comes from.
So far, I have been to Spain, Italy and France. I would absolutely love to go to Australia to experience the culture and the really different wildlife they have over there.

Spain

As my 2 cousins and aunt are from Barcelona, I tend to visit them once every year or two. I absolutely love walking down the Rambles shopping through the markets.
The last time I travelled to Barcelona i did some sight-seeing and i saw La Sagrida Familia which was amazing.

Italy

In fourth year I went to Italy skiing with my school. I didnt expect it to be so expensive!
We went to a place called Folgaria, it was absolutely baltic!
The people were really lovely but the cuisine was not for me, luckily I really enjoyed the skiing.

France

When I was 8, I went to Disneyland with my mom and my best friend kellieanne.
We stayed in the Adventurer's Hotel right next to Disneyland, It was an amazing experience.
i would definitely go back again.
We stayed for 4 nights and went to paris on the second day to visit the Eifell Tower.
